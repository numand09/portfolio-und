About Me

Born in Samsun, I've always been passionate about games and technology. As a gamer, developer, and athlete, I strive for constant self-improvement. I'm also curious about fields like history, geography, and science.

Game development is my creative and analytical outlet. I started with Godot and now primarily use Unreal Engine, with strong experience in Unity as well. I focus on applying SOLID principles and object-oriented programming throughout my work.

My professional journey includes academic projects like Revenge: The Dagger, commercial work at AnkA Interactive on Shahmaran, and development with AI systems at Welfish Studio.
